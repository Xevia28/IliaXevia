Project Overview

Roamio is a student-focused trip booking platform crafted to simplify group travel planning by making it social, collaborative, and hassle-free. It combines tools for group coordination, destination exploration, and seamless trip management into a single, user-friendly platform. Whether organizing a weekend getaway or a longer adventure, Roamio helps students collaborate effortlessly, discover exciting locations, and keep all travel details in one place—reducing stress and enhancing the fun of planning trips together.

Our student trip planning app, Roamio, empowers users to explore exciting destinations, create detailed itineraries, and book trips with ease—all while collaborating in real time with their group. Featuring tools for cost splitting and access to curated, student-friendly options, Roamio streamlines the entire planning process. By minimizing stress and enhancing coordination, it makes group travel more accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for students looking to make the most of their adventures together.
